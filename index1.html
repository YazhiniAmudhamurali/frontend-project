ABOUT OUR CITY COMPLAINT WEBSITE

Welcome to TN CITY COMPLAINT - PORTAL's premier platform for community feedback and civic engagement! Our website serves as a centralized hub for residents to voice their concerns and report grievances related to various public services and infrastructure in our city.



OUR MISSION

At TN CITY COMPLAINT - PORTAL Complaints, our mission is simple: to empower residents to make their voices heard and drive positive change in our community. We believe that by providing a platform for open communication between citizens and local authorities, we can work together to address issues and improve the quality of life for everyone in our city.



WHAT WE OFFER

Our website offers a user-friendly interface where residents can easily submit complaints and feedback regarding a wide range of issues, including but not limited to:


ROAD MAINTENANCE AND POTHOLE REPAIRS

WATER SUPPLY DISRUPTIONS OR QUALITY CONCERNS

ELECTRICITY OUTAGES AND INFRASTRUCTURE ISSUES

DRAINAGE SYSTEM PROBLEMS AND FLOODING

STREET LIGTNING MALFUNCTION

SANITATION AND WASTE MANAGEMENT ISSUES



HOW TO USE THIS PORTAL

The process of submitting a complaint through our website is simple and straightforward:


  1. Register on Our Website: The first step is to create an account by registering on our website. Provide your basic details to create your account.

  2. Login to Your Account: Once registered, log in to your account using your credentials.

  3. Choose Complaint Type: Select the type of complaint you want to report from the available options.

  4. Fill Out Complaint Form: After selecting the complaint type, you'll be directed to a complaint form specific to your chosen category. Fill out the required information, including the location details and a description of the problem.

  5. Submit Your Complaint: Review the information you've provided and then submit the form. Your complaint will be received by our team for further processing.


GET INVOLVED

We encourage all residents to actively participate in our community feedback process. Whether you're reporting a problem or supporting fellow residents by commenting on their complaints, your engagement helps us create a more responsive and accountable local government.
